special notations in algebra


  • •

    [G:H],   index (http://planetmath.org/Coset) of a subgroup H in the group G

  • •

    H◁G,   H is a normal subgroup of G

  • •

    G/H,   quotient group of G with respect to the normal subgroup H

  • •

    R/I or R-I,   quotient ring or difference ring of R with respect to the ideal I

  • •

    Z⁡(G),   center of the group G

  • •

    R⁢[G],   group ring of the group G over the ring R

  • •

    R⁢[α],   ring adjunction

  • •

    K⁢(α),   field adjunction

  • •

    S-1⁢R,   localization of the ring R at S

  • •

    R𝔭,   localization of the ring R at the complement of the prime ideal 𝔭

  • •

    (R,𝔪),   local ring R with its maximal ideal 𝔪

  • •

    MR,   a left R-module (http://planetmath.org/Module) M

  • •

    MR,   a right R-module (http://planetmath.org/Module) M

  • •

    K/k,   field extension where K is an extension field of k

  • •

    [K:k],   degree (http://planetmath.org/ExtensionField) of the field extension K/k

  • •

    K¯,   algebraic closure of a field K

  • •

    R⁢α1+…+R⁢αn  or  ⟨α1,…,αn⟩,   left ideal of the ring R generated by (http://planetmath.org/IdealGeneratedByASet) α1, …, αn

  • •

    A1≅A2,   isomorphism (http://planetmath.org/Isomorphism) between the systems (http://planetmath.org/AlgebraicSystem) A1 and A2
